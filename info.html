Location الموقع

OC Fairgrounds & Pacific Amphitheatre

OC Fair & Event Center
88 Fair Drive
Costa Mesa, CA 92626

Exit 405 at Fairview. Pacific Amphitheatre sits on the east side of the grounds. Main entrance on Fair Drive; accessible / family gate on Arlington.

Parking موقف السيارات

On-site, day-of, $20

On-site parking is $20 per day, cashless, available day-of. Lots open 90 minutes before gates each day. Attendants direct overflow to the adjacent Fairview lot once main lots fill.

Rideshare: dedicated Uber/Lyft zone on Fair Drive (south gate). Fastest way in and out on Saturday night — highly recommended after 8 PM.

Accessibility & ADA الوصول للجميع

Built to be enjoyed by everyone

  • Paved paths throughout · ADA restrooms at every amenity node
  • Reserved ADA seating zones at the Pacific Amphitheatre, sight-lined
  • Companion ticket at no additional charge
  • Continuous ADA cart service, 5 PM–midnight
  • ASL interpretation at PAC headliner sets — 72-hour notice at our contact form
  • Sensory-friendly quiet room at the First Aid node
  • Service animals welcome with handler documentation
What to Bring ما تحضر

The allowed list

  • Small clear bags or clutches, max 12"×12"
  • Small backpacks (subject to search at the gate)
  • Empty water bottles — refill stations at every corner
  • Sunscreen, hats, sunglasses — you'll want them
  • Portable phone chargers (with cables)
  • A light layer for after sundown
What's Not Allowed ممنوعات

Leave these at home

  • Weapons of any kind — no exceptions, including concealed carry
  • Illegal substances · cannabis (per California public-venue law)
  • Glass bottles or containers
  • Fireworks, lasers, flares
  • Drones & any remote-operated aerial devices
  • Professional cameras with detachable lenses (without a media pass)
  • Selfie sticks longer than 12"
  • Outside food & beverage (medical and dietary exceptions welcome)
  • Hookah · large coolers · umbrellas with pointed tips
Weather الطقس

Rain or shine — and it won't rain

Average June temperatures in Costa Mesa: 73°F high, 61°F low. The festival runs rain or shine. Bring a light layer for after sundown. In the unlikely event of severe weather, live updates post to Instagram and SMS subscribers.

Kids الأطفال

All ages welcome

Children three and under are free. Under 10 are free with a ticketed adult. The grounds are all-ages until 10 PM; after 11 PM the Indoor Stage becomes 18+. Strollers and wagons welcome — free parking at the East entry.

Pets الحيوانات

Service animals only

Pets of any kind are not permitted on the festival grounds. Service animals are welcome with handler documentation. Please leave everyone else at home — it will be hot, loud, and crowded.

Entry & Re-entry الدخول

Getting in — and back in

Valid government ID required for entry. Tickets are digital; screenshots accepted. Your wristband is your pass for the day — don't cut it off, don't lose it. Re-entry is allowed same-day with wristband intact.

  • Main Gate (Fair Drive): general entry
  • ADA / Family Gate (Arlington): accessible, stroller-friendly, rideshare drop-off
  • Line speeds: under 10 min before 5 PM · plan accordingly after 6 PM on Sat/Sun
Prayer & Quiet الصلاة

Space for a moment of stillness

A dedicated prayer room is open throughout festival hours, with wudu facilities adjacent. Prayer mats and qibla direction provided. A separate sensory-friendly quiet room — for anyone who needs a break from the crowd — is available at the First Aid node.

Jummah · Friday 1:00 PM Led by a local imam. Prayer rugs provided. Open to all.
Payment الدفع

Cashless grounds

All food, drink, merch, and vendor purchases are cashless. We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Cash App. ATMs are available near the Main Gate and the Food Court. Outside the wine & beer garden (21+, west side), the rest of the grounds are alcohol-free.

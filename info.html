Hours

Gates, stages, and closing time

ساعات المهرجان

  • Friday, June 19: Gates open 5:00 PM · Festival Stage 5:30 PM–11:00 PM · Indoor Stage 8:00 PM–1:00 AM
  • Saturday, June 20: Gates open 2:00 PM · Festival Stage 2:00 PM–midnight · PAC Amphitheatre 7:00 PM–11:00 PM · Indoor Stage 3:00 PM–2:00 AM
  • Sunday, June 21: Gates open 2:00 PM · Festival Stage 2:00 PM–11:00 PM · PAC Amphitheatre 7:00 PM–11:00 PM · Indoor Stage 3:00 PM–midnight

Last entry is 10:00 PM each night. Wristband required for re-entry — don't lose it.

Ages

All ages welcome

لكل الأعمار

This is a festival for the whole family — your friends, your parents, your Teta. The grounds are all-ages every day. Children three and under are free and don't need a ticket.

Note on the Indoor Stage: Late-night programming (after 11 PM) is 18+. All other stages remain all-ages for the full day.

Entry & Re-entry

Getting in — and back in

الدخول

Valid government ID required for entry. Tickets are digital; screenshots are accepted. Your wristband is issued at the gate and is your access for the day — don't cut it off, don't lose it.

  • Main Gate (Fair Drive): general entry
  • ADA / Family Gate (Arlington): accessible entry, stroller-friendly, ride-share drop-off
  • Re-entry allowed same day with wristband intact
  • Line speeds: under 10 minutes before 5 PM, plan accordingly after 6 PM on Sat/Sun

Bag Policy

What you can bring in

الحقائب

Allowed: small clear bags or clutches (max 12"×12"), small backpacks subject to search, empty water bottles (water stations on site), sunscreen, portable phone chargers.

Not allowed: professional cameras with detachable lenses, large coolers, outside food & beverage (except for medical or dietary needs), umbrellas with pointed tips.

Prohibited Items

Leave at home

ممنوعات

  • Weapons of any kind — no exceptions, including concealed carry
  • Illegal substances
  • Glass bottles or containers
  • Fireworks, lasers, flares
  • Drones & any remote-operated aerial devices
  • Selfie sticks longer than 12"
  • Pets (service animals welcome with documentation)

Payment

Cashless grounds

الدفع الإلكتروني

All food, drink, merch, and vendor purchases on the grounds are cashless. We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Cash App. ATMs are available near the Main Gate and Food Court if needed.

ADA & Accessibility

Built to be enjoyed by everyone

الوصول للجميع

The grounds are fully ADA-compliant. Paved paths throughout. Accessible restrooms at every amenity node. Sign language interpretation at PAC Amphitheatre headliner sets — notify us 72 hours in advance at our access email.

  • ADA seating zones at the Pacific Amphitheatre — reserved, sight-lined
  • Companion ticket at no additional charge
  • Continuous ADA cart service across the grounds (5 PM–midnight)
  • Sensory-friendly quiet room at the First Aid node
  • Service animals welcome with handler documentation

Medical

First aid & medical

إسعاف أولي

Staffed medical tent on site every day, open from gates to close. Emergency medical services on standby. Hydration stations across the grounds — use them, it's Southern California in June. Nursing and diaper-change space is inside the Family Lounge.

Prayer & Quiet

Space for a moment of stillness

مساحة الصلاة

A dedicated prayer room is open throughout festival hours, with wudu facilities adjacent. Prayer mats and qibla direction provided. The sensory-friendly quiet room — for anyone who needs a break from the crowd — is separate and always available.

Family

The Family Lounge

جناح العائلة

Shaded seating, cooler air, nursing space, diaper changing, stroller parking, and a kids' craft corner. Free, open all day. It's a place to regroup, feed the baby, and not lose anyone.

Smoking

Designated areas only

التدخين

Smoking and vaping are permitted only in clearly marked outdoor designated zones. Cannabis consumption of any kind is not permitted anywhere on the grounds, per California public-venue law. Hookah is not permitted.

Weather

Rain or shine

الطقس

The festival runs rain or shine. Average June temperatures in Costa Mesa: 73°F high, 61°F low. Bring a light layer for after sundown. In the unlikely event of severe weather, live updates post to our Instagram and SMS subscribers — sign up on the contact page.

Lost & Found

Lost something?

مفقودات

Lost & Found is at the Main Gate information booth. Items are collected nightly and held for 30 days after the festival. Reach our Lost & Found team through the contact page if you need to report something after you've left.

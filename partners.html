The difference

Sponsor or partner?

Two different relationships — both essential. Sponsors fund the festival and receive brand activation in return. Partners contribute reach, cultural authority, or resources in a values-aligned collaboration.

Sponsor · الراعي

A commercial partnership.

Paid sponsors invest financially in the festival and receive brand visibility, activation space, on-site presence, content rights, and tickets in return. Best fit for brands targeting the MENA-American audience.

  • Financial investment in the festival
  • Brand activation & logo placement
  • On-site booth or branded space
  • Four tiers: Presenting · Platinum · Gold · Supporting
See sponsor tiers →
Partner · الشريك

A community partnership.

Partners are community organizations, media outlets, nonprofits, and institutional allies. They contribute networks, reach, editorial coverage, or programming alignment — not a sponsorship fee.

  • No financial contribution required
  • Cross-promotion with aligned audiences
  • Access to festival-produced content
  • Co-programmed affiliate events & side-programs

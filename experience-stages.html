The Stages · المسارح

ثلاثة مسارح Three stages. Three tempos. One weekend.

Every stage is built for a different moment of the night. The Pacific Amphitheatre is the main event — the headline voices on the biggest sound system we can put in the ground. The Festival Stage is the all-day outdoor pulse. The Indoor Stage is where the evening goes when the headliners end and the night is still open.

PAC Amphitheatre at night · hero shot

Main Stage · المدرج الرئيسي

Pacific Amphitheatre

المدرج الرئيسي

The 8,500-seat amphitheatre inside the fairgrounds — one of Southern California's most storied outdoor music venues. Curved seating, line-array sound, sightlines engineered for every section. Saturday and Sunday nights, this is where the headliners land.

Emerging + Late Night · المسرح الداخلي

The Indoor Stage

المسرح الداخلي

The festival's second act. A 400-capacity indoor room built for intimate sets by emerging MENA voices, poetry showcases, late-night DJs, and afterparties that run past when the headliners have said goodnight. All-ages before 11 PM, 18+ after.

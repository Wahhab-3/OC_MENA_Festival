For the parents · للأهل

We thought of the small things.

This is a family festival because MENA is a family culture. Every service you need to bring a four-year-old and a grandmother through a ten-hour festival day is on the grounds — and most of it is free.

  • Kids under 10 freeWith a ticketed adult. No limit — bring the whole family.
  • Stroller & wagon parkingFree, staffed, by the East Entry. Wagons available to rent.
  • Prayer roomsTwo quiet rooms with wudu facilities near the bazaar center. Jummah at 1 PM Friday.
  • Nursing roomsPrivate, air-conditioned, with changing tables. Family Services building.
  • Lost kid protocolWristbands at every entry with your phone number. Central info booth = reunion point.
  • Sensory-friendly hourSunday 11 AM – noon. Rides running slow, volume down. For neurodivergent kids and families.
  • ADA accessibilityFull ADA pathways, reserved seating at every stage, accessible parking, sign language interpretation at headliners.

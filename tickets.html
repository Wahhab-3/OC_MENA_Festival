Official box office only

All tickets are sold through the OC Fair & Event Center's official ticketing partner. Buying from resellers or unverified social posts puts you at risk of counterfeit wristbands. If it's not on ocmenafestival.com or the fairgrounds box office — it's not real.

What a festival ticket includes

Every festival day pass includes full grounds access, the Festival Stage, the Indoor Stage, the bazaar, food court, artisan souq, rides, and family lounge. The only thing it does not include is a reserved seat at the Pacific Amphitheatre — that's the upgrade.

Why the Amphitheatre is separate

The Pacific Amphitheatre is an 8,500-seat venue inside the fairgrounds with reserved seating. We've structured ticketing so every Amp seat guarantees a sightline — and so festival-only guests still get an incredible weekend at a fair price.

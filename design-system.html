Internal design system. نظام

The working token set + component inventory for the OC MENA Festival site. Every screen on the festival build is composed from these primitives. Three rules override everything; the rest is atmosphere.

Rule 1. No pure black — ever. --ink (#281801) is the deepest tone on the site.
Rule 2. No 1px borders. Sections separate by tonal shift in the background, not lines.
Rule 3. Aldhabi always renders at 1.7× the Latin neighbor. Non-negotiable.

Color لون

The page stacks cream → sand → sand-deep for depth. Red is energy, maroon is heritage, gold is punctuation — never a surface.

The Aldhabi ١٫٧× rule

Aldhabi glyphs render optically smaller than their nominal em-size. Every Aldhabi instance ships at 1.7× the size of its Latin neighbor. The .ar utility class bakes this in.

Without rule — broken

Three days. الوطن

Arabic glyph renders at the same nominal size as the Latin — visually 40% smaller, unreadable, looks like a font-fallback bug.
With 1.7× rule — correct

Three days. الوطن

font-size: 1.7em · line-height: 1
Aldhabi paired with Newsreader at 1.7× — visually matches the Latin weight and earns its place beside it.

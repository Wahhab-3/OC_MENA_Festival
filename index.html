What is MENA · ماذا يعني مينا

From Morocco to the Gulf — a region with one heartbeat.

MENA is the Middle East and North Africa. Twenty-two countries. Hundreds of dialects. One of the oldest musical traditions on earth — and one of the most alive right now. This festival brings it home to Orange County.

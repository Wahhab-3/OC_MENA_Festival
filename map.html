Parking & ride-share

Official lots are on Fair Drive, Arlington Drive, and the West Gate. Ride-share pickup and drop-off at the ADA / Family Gate — it's the fastest way in and out all three nights, especially after headliner sets.

Getting around

The grounds are walkable end-to-end in about twelve minutes. A shuttle loop runs between the Amphitheatre and the Main Gate every fifteen minutes from 5 PM until midnight. ADA cart service is continuous.

Day-of navigation

The real-time map — with set times, crowd density, nearest food stall, nearest restroom, and live ride-share queue — opens on June 15. Bookmark this page. It'll update automatically.
